Description:

This website is directed to businesswomen of 18 +, that own or run business located or offering services in Ibagué Colombia, interested in to join a community of powerful women building commercial business. There is not a minimum income required. With a device able to connect to Internet is possible join our community which provides a net of local businesswomen looking to make our business more visible, collaborate in services offered, teach each other strategies of growth, share interesting information and process before government entities' opportunities and the fulfillment of our rights.
